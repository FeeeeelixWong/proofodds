# Third-party attribution

## TxLINE IDL and integration model

`server/idl/txoracle.json` is the official TxLINE devnet Anchor IDL sourced from:

https://github.com/txodds/tx-on-chain

The repository is distributed under the Apache License 2.0. ProofOdds uses the IDL to construct read-only `validateFixture` and `validateStatV2` calls and follows the public examples for PDA derivation and free-tier activation.

ProofOdds is an independent hackathon project and is not endorsed by TxODDS.
